Overview

This site was designed to provide simple answers to complex questions to your CrossFit Open-related queries. This tool is a simple, web-based interface to allowing the user to implement various methods to explore data from the CrossFit Open and answer questions about how your performance relates to other athletes around the world.

The data are on this site reflect the submitted RX scores on the CrossFit Games website. No cleaning or additional processing of the data is done on this site; the data are purely as submitted by athletes participating in the Open. The site is free and is unaffiliated with CrossFit.
